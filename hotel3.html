nitk beach with our guide

NITK Beach, located near the National Institute of Technology Karnataka (NITK) in Surathkal, Mangalore, is a popular coastal destination. Known for its serene environment, clean sands, and scenic beauty, the beach offers a peaceful retreat from the bustling city life. It's a favorite spot for students, locals, and tourists alike, providing picturesque views of the Arabian Sea and stunning sunsets. A notable landmark on the beach is the lighthouse, which offers panoramic views of the coastline. The beach's proximity to the institute makes it a convenient and cherished spot for relaxation and leisure activities.
